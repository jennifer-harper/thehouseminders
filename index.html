A Professional Standard of Care

In an era of faceless apps and "random" contractors, we offer a boutique alternative built on security, maturity, and local accountability. Whether you need a reliable house sitter or a dedicated cat minder, we provide a service that prioritizes the safety of your home and the well-being of your pets.

Already your neighbours

We live and work in the Western Hills. We know the wind in Kelburn, the micro-climates of Ngaio/Khandallah, and the quiet streets of Karori. Being local means we are never far away and can be truly responsive to the unique needs of homes in our area.

Active Home Security

We don’t just feed your cat; we manage your risk. By ensuring mail is tucked away, bins are moved on schedule, and lighting patterns vary, we maintain the "lived-in" look that is the best deterrent for unwanted attention.

Cat Companionship

We understand that cats are family. We provide a calm, consistent presence and genuine affection, ensuring their daily routines remain undisturbed in the comfort of their own territory. We love sending snapshots so you can see how they’re doing.

Plant Care

Whether it’s a collection of delicate indoor tropicals or an established hillside garden, we provide the attentive care your greenery needs. We’re happy to follow your specific watering schedules and feeding routines to ensure your plants stay lush and healthy while you’re away.

Clear Communication

Our Service Tiers

Every home and pet is unique. Following our initial consultation, we provide a transparent, all-inclusive proposal tailored to your specific dates and requirements. Below are our starting packages, which serve as a foundation for our custom care plans.

The Daily Visit

From $40 per visit

Perfect for the independent cat or the well-tended garden. We provide a 30-minute window for a range of dedicated services including feeding, fresh water, and litter maintenance, a security sweep of the property, mail collection, and ensuring your indoor plants are thriving.

The Double ($70): Morning and evening visits for pets who appreciate a routine and homes that benefit from an active "lived-in" presence.

The In-Residence Stay

From $95 per night

For pets who prefer company and homes that shouldn't be left overnight. We provide a 12-hour onsite presence (7pm–7am), offering your pets companionship and your home a high level of security.

This is the preferred choice for owners of senior pets, those requiring medication, or properties with high-value art and assets. This service also ensures your property remains occupied for insurance compliance while you’re away.

The Bespoke Residency

By Private Consultation

For high-specification homes or extended absences, we offer a full-time residency model. Designed for exceptional properties in areas like Khandallah, Kelburn, or Karori, we provide a dedicated 24/7 presence tailored to your home’s unique requirements.

This tier is fully customizable to your needs. Services range from specialized pet care protocols and garden maintenance to concierge requests such as:

Arrival Prep: Fridge stocking, click-and-collect groceries, or preparing an arrival meal.

Fresh-Start Service: Ensuring all rooms have fresh linens and the home is "guest-ready" for your return.

Property Logistics: Managing deliveries, providing supervised access for cleaners, and liaising with service contractors or trades.
